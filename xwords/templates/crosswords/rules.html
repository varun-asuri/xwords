Rules for American-style crosswords

Duplicate Words

Duplicate words are not allowed in American-style crosswords regardless of orientation.

Word Length

All words must be at least three letters long and not include any punctuation.

Word Keying

All letters in the crossword must appear in exactly one horizontal word and exactly one vertical word.

Connected Crossword

All spaces in the crossword must be reachable by every other space in the puzzle using only horizontal and vertical steps.

Crossword Symmetry

The blocks for all crosswords follow a 180 degree rotational symmetry, meaning for every block, there is another block exactly opposite to it.
